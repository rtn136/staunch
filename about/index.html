Ganesh Kalburgi
Founder

Ganesh Kalburgi is a seasoned professional with over 15 years of experience across diverse corporate sectors. His expertise spans US mortgage services, customer service, client relations, and project management, along with successfully leading various business teams. Ganesh's career includes notable positions at leading companies such as SLK Global, Altisource, and Ocwen, where he developed a strong foundation in industry standards and operational excellence. Mano Bharath RC
